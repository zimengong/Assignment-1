Woodlands Waterfront Park

Woodlands Waterfront Park is well-known for its tranquility and close proximity to Malaysia's Johor Bahru.
This scenic coastal park offers an attractive and panaromic view of the causeway and the Straits of Johor. In addition, Woodlands Waterfront Park not only accomodates a wide range of equiments that both the young and old can enjoy, but it is also home to a variety of fauna and fiona.

Park Information





Get as close as you can to our neighbouring country Malaysia at Woodlands Waterfront! Located in the north of Singapore, the park is situated at the edge of Singapore, making it the furthest point away from the mainland. The 11-hectare park houses a variety of equipments that cater to the young and old, ideal for recreational activities and family bonding.
